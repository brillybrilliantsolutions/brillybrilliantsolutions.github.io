Brilly's Brilliant Solutions

Founded in 2002 by Ian Brill, a proud Central Queensland local with over 45 years' experience in the industry, Brilly's has grown to a team of more than a dozen and can provide brilliant solutions to roofing projects across Central Queensland. Brilly's team thinks outside the square to ensure a quality result every time. Brilly's takes care of any type of project: 

  • Residential
  • Commercial
  • Industrial
  • Government
  • Heritage-listed

and many more! Brilly's works with and is experienced across a variety of materials and levels of complexity.

Ian Brill
Director

Born and raised in Bouldercome, Central Queensland, Ian completed his Plumbing apprenticeship with the State Works Department in 1978. After spending a few years building mining communities across Western QLD, he returned to Rockhampton with his growing family in 1983 to work for the local Solahart franchise, where he remained for the next 19 years. Sensing an opportunity to use his skills on a grander scale, Ian founded Brilly's Brilliant Solutions to provide high quality roofing solutions for locals, by locals. 

Clint Vesey
