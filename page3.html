This project is up there with one of the most complex and riskiest projects Brilly's has undertaken. With 10,000m2 of 60m long underslung Aramax sheeting hoisted into position over the top of a Boeing 747, Beoing 707 and a constellation aeroplane.

Blackall hospital is made up of 1800m2 of Aramax Mansard roofing sheets and 2000m2 of wall cladding.
